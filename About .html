ABOUT Muniss Research Foundation

The Muniss Research Foundation, Chennai is one of the largest and fastest growing professional associations in India for promoting research in the fields of engineering, technology and science. The Muniss Research Foundation is a leading organization in the field of rapid technological revolution and sustainable development in the field of engineering, technology and science by facilitating the flow of knowledge from one company to another and from one industry to another.

The Ace system is supported by its International Advisory Board and which is not limited to the subcontinent, but we have geniuses from the Middle East and Europe to move our organization forward. The Muniss Research Foundation, in collaboration with leading technology institutes including universities and research centers, promotes the transfer of technical knowledge among student fathers. Today, the Muniss Research Foundation is at the forefront of publishing research articles in high-quality, peer-reviewed, indexed journals. The mission of the Muniss Research Foundation is to develop and conduct joint interdisciplinary research on sophisticated methods and technologies within its expert areas. Our organization has a strong network of scholars and practitioners from India and abroad who are further strengthened by conducting conferences, publications and engineering projects. The Muniss Research Foundation has organized scientific activities and conferences in collaboration with colleges of science and engineering and universities such as Saveetha University and Ghazi University.
